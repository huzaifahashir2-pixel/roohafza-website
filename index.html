THE SECRET FORMULA

Natural Ingredients

Rooh Afza is made from a unique recipe of botanical ingredients, carefully selected for their refreshing and therapeutic cooling properties.

Rose (Gul-e-Surkh)

Provides the signature deep-red hue and floral aroma, offering immediate mental refreshment and cardiac cooling.

Mint (Pudina)

Infuses a burst of crisp coolness that aids digestion, cools the stomach, and alleviates heat exhaustion.

Kewra (Screw Pine)

An exotic distillate that delivers an enchanting scent and serves as an ancient remedy for heat strokes.

Water Lily (Neelofar)

Renowned in traditional Unani medicine for regulating body temperature and soothing internal heat.
